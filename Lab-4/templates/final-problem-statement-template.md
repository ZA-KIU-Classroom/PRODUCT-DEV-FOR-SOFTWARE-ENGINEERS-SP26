## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Statement is about a problem, not a solution (does not mention your product)
- [ ] WHO is specific enough that you could find these people on campus today
- [ ] WHAT describes observable behavior or failure, not a feeling
- [ ] WHEN/WHERE provides situational context
- [ ] WHY identifies root cause, not surface symptom
- [ ] IMPACT includes at least one quantified number from interviews
- [ ] Cites 8 or more interviews with specific evidence
- [ ] A stranger reading this would understand the problem without further explanation
- [ ] The problem is real (people actually have it), painful (they care enough to act), and underserved (current solutions are inadequate)
