## If You Are Stuck

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Not enough interviews | Emergency catch-up in first 30 min. Use `emergency-interview-template.md`. |
| Cannot find patterns | Extract more notes. Aim for 50+. Look for quotes that sound similar across interviews. |
| Problem statement too vague | Add specific numbers from interviews. Use exact ICP language. Cite more interviews. |
| Do not know root cause | Keep asking "why" until you cannot go deeper. See `guides/five-whys-guide.md`. |
