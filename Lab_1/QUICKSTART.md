## 3H Brainstorm (individual, silent, 25 minutes)

Write **one problem per H**. Real observations only. No hypotheticals.

**Hairband** -- A problem in your own daily life you work around.

**Headache** -- A problem you have watched someone else experience firsthand.

**Heartburn** -- A recurring problem in a community or context you know well.

**Format for each:**
> WHO has trouble with WHAT, WHEN/WHERE, and it matters because WHY.

---

## STOP. Read This Before the Sharing Round.
