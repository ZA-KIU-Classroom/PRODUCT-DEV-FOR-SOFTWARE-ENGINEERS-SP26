## Opening (2 to 3 minutes)

"Thank you for speaking with me today. My name is [name] and I am working on a student research project at Kutaisi International University. We are trying to understand [problem space in plain language -- no jargon, no mention of your solution].

This is not a sales call and there is nothing to sign up for. I just want to learn from your experience.

There are no right or wrong answers. If I ask something that does not make sense, just say so.

With your permission I would like to take notes. Is that okay?

Let us start. Can you tell me a little about [your role / your daily routine / how you currently handle X]?"

## Closing (1 to 2 minutes)

"We are coming up on time. Two last questions.

Is there anything about [topic] that you think I should have asked but did not?

Is there anyone else you know who deals with this that you think I should speak with?"

Thank the participant. Do not pitch your solution. If they ask what you are building: "We are still in the research phase -- we want to understand the problem fully before deciding what to build."
