## Instructions for feedback givers

You are acting as judges, not supporters. Your job is to give feedback that helps the presenting team improve before Demo Day. Be direct. Be specific. Do not soften feedback to the point of uselessness.

The rule: one thing that worked well (be specific about which moment and why it worked), two things to improve (name the slide number, the claim, or the moment, and say exactly what you would change).

You have 3 minutes to discuss and complete this form after the pitch ends.
