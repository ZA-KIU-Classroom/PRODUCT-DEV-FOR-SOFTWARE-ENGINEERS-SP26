# One-Pager Template

**Team name:** [Your team name]
**Product name:** [Your product name]
**Date:** Thursday 4 June 2026 or Friday 5 June 2026

This template produces the content for your one-pager. Build the actual designed PDF in Google Slides, Canva, or Figma using this content. Export as `one-pager.pdf` and commit to `05-fundraising/`.

The one-pager must fit on a single A4 page when printed at 100% scale. Minimum font size: 10pt body, 8pt labels. Maximum word count across all sections: 300 words.

## Design checklist before exporting

- [ ] All content fits on one A4 page at 100% scale
- [ ] Font size is 10pt or larger for all body text
- [ ] Live URL is the most visually prominent element after the product name
- [ ] No paragraph is longer than 3 lines
- [ ] All numbers match your analytics dashboard pulled today
- [ ] Team photo(s) are professional (not selfies or casual photos if avoidable)
- [ ] The document looks clean when printed in black and white (test this)
- [ ] File is exported as PDF
- [ ] PDF is named exactly `one-pager.pdf`
- [ ] PDF is committed to `05-fundraising/one-pager.pdf`
- [ ] Two printed copies are prepared for Demo Day
