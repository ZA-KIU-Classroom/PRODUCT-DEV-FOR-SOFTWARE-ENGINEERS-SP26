## The brief

A 60-second vertical video shot on any smartphone. A complete stranger must understand what your product does and why they should care after watching it once. No slides. No voiceover narration layered over a screen recording. At least one team member must appear on camera.

Think TikTok or Instagram Reel, not a corporate explainer video. Energy and clarity beat production quality. A shaky authentic take in the right location beats a polished studio recording with a weak script.

**Hard requirements:**

- 60 seconds maximum. Judges stop watching at 62 seconds.
- At least one team member on camera for at least 10 seconds.
- Your live product shown on a real device for at least 15 seconds.
- Committed to `09-final/demo-video.md` as a link or file path before 11 June at 23:59.
- Linked from your `README.md`.
