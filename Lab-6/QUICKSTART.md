## If Something Goes Wrong

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Team cannot agree on MVP scope | Apply the boundary test: does the user need this to reach the activation moment? If no, it is out of scope. PO decides after 10 minutes of debate. |
| Cannot estimate a story | If the team cannot estimate confidently, mark it as ? and flag it as a spike. Do not guess a number you do not believe. |
| Story does not have interview evidence | Move it off the MVP backlog into a future phase. Do not build things without evidence. |
| Sprint 1 commitment is too high | Cut the lowest-priority stories. It is better to over-deliver on 8 points than under-deliver on 14. |
| Running out of time in Part 4 | Prioritise AC for activation-flow stories first. Every other story can have AC written before Lab 8. |
