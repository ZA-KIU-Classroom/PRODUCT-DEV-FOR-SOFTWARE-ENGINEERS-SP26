## Primary ICP: [Problem 1 name]

This ICP describes the person most likely to experience your committed problem most acutely. They are your primary interview target.

### 1. Segment

Who is this person? Be specific about role, year, institution, and relevant characteristics. You should be able to identify them within a few minutes of conversation.

[Write here]

**Test:** Could you walk into a campus building and within 10 minutes identify someone matching this description? If not, narrow it further.

---

### 2. Context

When and where does the problem occur? What is the specific situation they are in when the pain is felt?

[Write here]

**Test:** Is this a specific enough context that you could design an interview question around it?

---

### 3. Primary Pain

What exactly goes wrong? Describe the felt experience of the problem — not the cause, but what the person experiences.

[Write here]

**Test:** Would someone matching this ICP read this description and say "that is exactly what it feels like"?

---

### 4. Current Workaround

What are they already doing to cope with this problem? Be specific about the tools, processes, or behaviours.

[Write here]

**If you cannot answer this, you have not done enough thinking yet. Do not leave this blank.**

---

### 5. Job to Be Done

What outcome is this person actually trying to achieve? Complete the sentence:

"When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [desired outcome]."

[Write here]

---

### 6. Success Signal

If this problem were solved, what would change for this person? What would they stop doing? What would they start feeling?

[Write here]

---

### 7. Where to Find Them

Where do people matching this ICP spend time? Name specific places, communities, groups, or channels where you can find and recruit interviewees.

[Write here]

**This must be specific enough to act on today. "On campus" is not specific enough.**

---

### 8. Why You, Why Now

What gives your team access or credibility with this person? Why would they give you 30 minutes of their time?

[Write here]
