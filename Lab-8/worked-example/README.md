# Worked Example -- StudySpace Finders

This folder contains a complete, filled-in growth strategy package using **StudySpace Finders** as the fictional team. Use it as a reference, not as a template to copy verbatim. Your team's product, channels, and numbers will differ. The structure and the level of detail are what to imitate.

---

## What StudySpace Finders Is

A web app that helps KIU computer science students find available, quiet study spaces on campus in real time. Users open the app, see which library rooms and lab spaces are currently free or in low use, and book a slot. The product was discovered, validated, and prototyped through Weeks 1 to 7 of this course. Sprint 1 began on 23 April. By Week 10, the team has a deployed MVP at studyspace-finders.vercel.app, an event schema firing in Mixpanel, and a live smoke test running on Reddit and the KIU CS Discord.

## How to Use the Worked Example

1. **Read all four documents in order** before opening the templates. They tell a connected story.
2. **Notice the level of specificity.** "We will post in r/KIU twice a week" is concrete. "We will use social media" is not.
3. **Notice every assumption has a source.** Even rough estimates have a one-sentence justification.
4. **Notice the honest acknowledgements.** The team says "we have no data yet on D30 retention; we use 40% as a placeholder based on similar campus apps and will replace by week 12." That honesty is what earns marks.
5. **Open the spreadsheet.** Toggle the scenario between Best, Expected, and Worst. Watch the numbers change. Your spreadsheet should behave the same way.

---

## What to Copy and What Not To Copy

**Copy:**
- The structure of each document.
- The level of justification per assumption.
- The honesty about uncertainty.
- The sensitivity analysis pattern.
- The spreadsheet column structure.

**Do not copy:**
- The channels themselves (your product is not StudySpace Finders).
- The numbers (your conversion rates, CAC, and LTV are different).
- The narrative voice if it does not fit your team.
